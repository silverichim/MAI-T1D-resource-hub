EPCOT Data

EPCOT v2 is a general AI model that predicts cell type-specific/context-specific regulatory signals from ATAC-seq data and DNA sequences.

Predicted Modalities

  • Nascent transcription signals (e.g., PRO-seq, GRO-seq, TT-seq, Bru-seq)
  • Gene expression profiles from total and polyA+ RNA-seq
  • Transcription factor binding and histone modifications
  • 3D chromatin organization, including Micro-C, Hi-C, and ChIA-PET contact maps
  • Transcription initiation signals (e.g., CAGE-seq, GRO-cap, PRO-cap)
  • Synthetic enhancer activity measured by STARR-seq
